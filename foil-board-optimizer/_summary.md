# Foil Board Internal Structure Optimizer

**Status:** `in-progress`

Autonomous topology optimization of hydrofoil board internal structures using FEA simulation and an AI-driven experimental outer loop, following the Karpathy autoresearch pattern. The optimizer places material where it's structurally needed along the load path from deck to mast mount, creating internal ribs, lattices, and channels rather than a simple hollow shell. Includes a SIMP topology optimizer, neural surrogate model, and STL export for 3D printing.
