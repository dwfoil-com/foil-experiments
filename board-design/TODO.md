# Board Design — To-Do / Ideas

## Design Ideas to Investigate

### Additive Manufacturing for Board Construction
- Explore additive manufacturing techniques for foil board production
- Could enable complex internal structures (lattice, topology-optimised infill) that aren't possible with traditional foam/glass layup
- Reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cUQJ1pEWKjA

### AI for Crystal Structure Design
- Investigate AI-driven crystal structure design for advanced board materials
- Potential for discovering novel material compositions with better strength-to-weight ratios or tunable flex characteristics
- Could tie into the FEA optimizer — let AI suggest both the shape and the material
- References:
  - https://www.vixiv.net/
  - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0h_0Eg7-bnk
  - https://youtu.be/PNiR2Cxc1iY?t=55
